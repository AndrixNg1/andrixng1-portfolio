## Mobile First Strategy

Designing for mobile requires extreme attention to space, typography, and touch targets. This mockup explores the constraints of smaller screens.

### Design Challenges Solved

- **Navigation**: Replacing complex desktop top-bars with intuitive bottom navigation or clean hamburger menus.
- **Touch Accessibility**: Ensuring all CTAs are thumb-friendly.
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Stacking elements logically without overwhelming the user's viewport.

## Conclusion

A seamless mobile experience that retains the premium "feel" of its desktop counterpart.
